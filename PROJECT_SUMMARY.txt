ALPHA 1: PAIRS TRADING STRATEGY
--------------------------------
Type: Statistical arbitrage / Mean reversion
Assets: BTC/ETH cryptocurrency futures
Logic: Cointegration-based spread trading

Entry Rules:
- Long spread when z-score < -2.0 (buy BTC, sell ETH)
- Short spread when z-score > +2.0 (sell BTC, buy ETH)

Exit Rules:
- Close position when |z-score| < 0.5 (mean reversion)
- Stop loss when |z-score| > 3.0 (cointegration break)

Parameters:
- Lookback: 60 days for hedge ratio calculation
- Rolling window: 20 days for z-score
- Position size: $1,000 per trade
- Market-neutral design
